Healthspan

Give us lifespan in good health, i.e., healthspan.

Let's aim to prolong strength, mobility, creativity, and mental acuity. Let's minimize disease, torpor, and depression. Here's seeking lifestyle choices and behaviors for which there is promising evidence for optimizing healthspan.

At this point, excercise (training), optimal weight and nutrition (eating), good sleep, and learning seem like themes offering most evidence and promise (that I've seen) for shaping healthspan positively.

Here's to the fun challenge of digesting available evidence and converting learning into improved results, a longer healthspan. It's all an experiment of n=1, but among other things, sharing here will give me a fun target around which to grow my python.
